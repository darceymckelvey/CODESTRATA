About CodeStrata

Explore the geological layers of your codebase, unearth hidden patterns, and understand code evolution.

Our Mission

CodeStrata was founded on the principle that complex codebases share many similarities with geological formations. Layers of code are built over time, with each layer serving a distinct purpose and containing artifacts of its development history.

Our mission is to provide developers with powerful tools to visualize, understand, and collaborate on code using geological metaphors. By making the invisible structures of code visible through geological mapping, we empower teams to make better architectural decisions.
